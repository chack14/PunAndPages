Detective Emma Griffin returns to her hometown to investigate a series of murders that appear disturbingly connected to her own past. As she delves into the case, she uncovers eerie secrets hidden in the woods and begins to suspect that someone close to her may be involved. This thriller is a chilling exploration of small-town secrets, familial ties, and the darkness lurking in familiar places. A.J. Rivers creates an atmospheric thriller with richly developed characters and a suspenseful plot. The themes of memory, fear, and loyalty run deep, intertwining Emma's personal journey with the case. The eerie woodland setting adds a haunting dimension, amplifying the tension and mystery. This novel is a perfect read for fans of suspenseful thrillers with layered, emotional depth.
