Years after being falsely accused of a brutal crime, a young woman attempts to rebuild her life while still seeking justice. Haunted by her past, she finds herself embroiled in a new mystery that brings her closer to uncovering the truth about her family. As she uncovers hidden connections and dangerous secrets, she realizes that the past may be far from buried. The story is a gripping journey of resilience, truth, and the quest for redemption. Charlie Donlea delivers a tightly-woven thriller, combining psychological tension with an intricate plot. Themes of memory, trauma, and justice resonate as the protagonist fights to clear her name and protect her future. Each twist adds layers of suspense, revealing both the strength and vulnerability of the human spirit. This compelling novel keeps readers on the edge, blending emotional depth with heart-pounding intrigue.
